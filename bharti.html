Course Plan

Over four weeks of live workshops and bonus on-demand content, Bharti will help you take control of your journey as a to-be mom.

Kickoff | Personal 1:1 Onboarding with Bharti

I want to get to know each of you personally before we jump into the 4 week course! Hence I will have a one-to-one onboarding call to understand your pregnancy and any specific concerns.

Week 1 | Pregnancy, Nutrition & Fitness [Live]

IIn the first class we will understand the key physical and hormonal changes that happen during the the 3 trimesters, common pregnancy discomforts and their home remedies, how to have a healthy pregnancy through right nutrition & fitness ( yes we will be moving in this session too! ) and the common warning signs to look out for.

Week 2 | Prenatal Yoga Core Strength [Live]

In this class, we will be practicing a holistic prenatal yoga routine that will help you to build core strength (focus areas - back, abdomen and pelvic floor) which is vital to have a healthy pain free pregnancy as well as faster recovery. We will end with a relaxing deep breathing & pranayama practice.

Week 3 | Birth Preparation, Labor & Delivery [Live]

This session is all about getting you physically & mentally prepared for labor and birth. We will be talking about Optimal fetal positioning, how to avoid tearing, understanding stages fo labor and when to go the hospital, labor pain management techniques for you and your partner, labor positions and medical pain relief options (epidural) that are generally available.

Week 4 | Prenatal Yoga - Hip Opening for Birth Preparation [Live]

This is another prenatal yoga session designed to help you open up the pelvis and hips to help engage the baby and prepare for natural birth. We will end with a relaxing positive visualisation practice to help you reduce anxiety and go in to your delivery with positive attitude ( and that is 90% of the game!)
